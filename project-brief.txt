## 1️⃣ Global UX Principles

### Content & Readability
*   **Typography:** High contrast. All non-heading text must be **Black (#000000 or #111111)**. No grey body text to ensure maximum readability.
*   **Background:** Clean **White (#FFFFFF)** background.
*   **Style:** Long-form, academic-style content is fully supported and encouraged.

### Layout
*   **Alignment:** Centered content with a max-width of **1140–1240px**.
*   **Spacing:** Clear vertical spacing to reduce cognitive load.
*   **Grouping:** Use card-based grouping sparingly, with **light grey** backgrounds only.

### Navigation Strategy
*   **Structure:** Multi-level navigation.
*   **Organization:** Courses are grouped by **program type** (e.g., Data Center, Risk), not marketing terms.
*   **Audience Segmentation:** Clear visual or navigational separation between:
    *   Individual Learners
    *   Corporate Clients
    *   Partners / Resellers

### Navbar Menu Structure
*   **Left:** Brand Logo (Home link).
*   **Center (Main Navigation):**
    *   **Programs** (Dropdown/Mega Menu):
        *   Data Center
        *   Business Continuity
        *   Cybersecurity
    *   **Corporate Services**
    *   **About Us**
    *   **Resources**
*   **Right (Utility & Action):**
    *   🔍 Search
    *   **Contact** (Text Link or Secondary Button)
    *   **Enquire / Apply** (Primary Button - Amber)

## 7️⃣ Marketing & Conversion Features

### 📅 Training Calendar (Global)
*   **Centralized View:** A dedicated page showing availability across all courses.
*   **Filters:** Filter by Date, Location, and Topic.
*   **Status Indicators:** "Guaranteed to Run" (Green), "Limited Seats" (Amber), "Sold Out" (Grey).

### 🎣 Lead Generation
*   **Brochure Downloads:** "Download Full Syllabus" (requires email capture).
*   **Waitlist:** Alert me when new dates are added (for sold-out/unscheduled items).
*   **Bulk Booking:** "Request a Team Quote" CTA distinct from individual enrolment.

### 🏆 Trust & Social Proof
*   **Alumni Showcase:** Logos of companies (e.g., Google, Amazon) where alumni work.
*   **Video Testimonials:** Embedded on About and Course pages.
*   **Urgency Banners:** Global sticky bar for upcoming deadlines or early-bird offers.

## 1️⃣1️⃣ UX Behaviour & Interactions

### 🧭 Navigation Mechanics
*   **Sticky Header:** The main navigation bar remains visible (sticky) as the user scrolls, potentially compacting slightly. To maximize screen real estate, it can hide on scroll down and reappear on scroll up.
*   **Mega Menu Delays:** Dropdown menus should have a slight delay (e.g., 300ms) before closing when the mouse leaves, preventing accidental closing during diagonal mouse movement.
*   **Mobile Menu:** A smooth slide-out drawer from the right side. The "Hamburger" icon transforms into an "X" close icon.

### ✨ Micro-interactions
*   **Buttons:** Primary buttons should have a subtle scale (98%) or background shade shift on click (active state) to provide tactile feedback. Hover states should lift slightly or brighten.
*   **Cards:** Course cards should have a subtle elevation lift (box-shadow expansion) on hover to indicate clickability.
*   **Links:** Text links in paragraphs should have a distinct underline animation (e.g., left-to-right underline growth) on hover.

### 🔄 Feedback Loops
*   **Form Validation:** Inline validation (Green check / Red cross) appears immediately after a field loses focus (onBlur). Error messages should be descriptive.
*   **Loading States:** Use "Skeleton Screens" (grey placeholders) for loading content (e.g., course lists) instead of generic spinners, to reduce perceived load time.
*   **Action Feedback:** When a form is submitted, the button should show a spinner, then transition to a "Success" state (e.g., "Sent!").

### 🎬 Transitions
*   **Page Transitions:** A very subtle fade-in transition when navigating between pages.
*   **Modals:** Use a backdrop blur with a gentle slide-up or fade-in animation for modal dialogs (e.g., "Request Quote").

## 1️⃣2️⃣ Prototype & Interactivity Requirements

### 🖱️ Clickable High-Fidelity Dummy
*   **No Dead Ends:** Every button, link, and interactive element MUST be clickable and lead to a relevant state or page.
*   **Deep Linking:**
    *   **Trainer Profiles:** Clicking a trainer's name/photo must open a detailed profile modal or page.
    *   **Schedule Selection:** Clicking a "Select Date" button must toggle an active state and update the pricing/summary section.
    *   **Course Drill-down:** Clicking a course card must lead to the full Course Detail page.

### 🚫 No "Empty States"
*   **Populated Data:** No section should display "Coming Soon" or empty placeholders.
    *   **Schedules:** All courses must have at least 3 future dates listed.
    *   **Testimonials:** Must use realistic text and photos, not "Lorem Ipsum".
    *   **Blog/Resources:** Must have at least 5 populated articles with thumbnails.
*   **Visual Completeness:** The prototype must feel like a live, production-ready site to the user.

### 📸 Visual Assets
*   **High-Quality Placeholders:** Use high-resolution dummy images from **Unsplash** or **Pexels**.
*   **Relevance:** Images must match the context (e.g., corporate meeting rooms for "Business Continuity", server racks for "Data Center").
*   **No Placeholders:** Avoid grey boxes with dimensions (e.g., "400x300"). use real images only.
